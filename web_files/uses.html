Uses


The Composi-Sleeve system is a non-welded pipe repair and reinforcing system that can be utilized to restore damaged or leaking pipes or tanks back to full design strength. The Composi-Sleeve system has many different applications including:



composi-sleeve for pipline repairs


  • Repairs dents, gouges, and corrosion anomolies
  • Protects exposed or potentially exposes pipelines at washes, perennial creeks, spans and bridges
